FAQ

Common Questions

What if my customer list is really old?

That's fine — most of our clients have lists that are 1–3 years old. We clean and segment the list first so we're only messaging people who are likely to respond. Older lists often surprise people with how many customers come back.

How many customers do I need on my list?

We can work with lists as small as 50 contacts. The bigger the list the more potential bookings, but even a small well-targeted list can generate significant revenue with the right offer.

Do I need to do anything once we start?

Very little. You share your customer list, approve the campaign copy, and then just answer the calls and messages that come in. We handle everything else.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients see responses within the first 24–48 hours of a campaign launching. Bookings typically start coming in within the first week. Results vary based on list size and offer, but the first campaign usually pays for itself.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contracts. All plans are month-to-month and you can cancel anytime. We keep clients by getting results, not by locking them in.

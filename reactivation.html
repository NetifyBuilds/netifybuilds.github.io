How It Works

The Full Process

From your first conversation with us to booked jobs in your calendar — here's exactly what happens.

01

You Share Your Contact List

Send us your existing customer database — even if it's years old. A simple spreadsheet or CRM export works fine. We'll take it from there.

02

We Clean & Segment the List

We remove duplicates, bad data, and organize contacts by how long they've been inactive. This lets us send the right message to the right person.

03

We Write the Campaign

We write custom SMS and email copy built around a seasonal offer — no generic templates. Every message is written specifically for your business and your customers.

04

We Launch & Monitor

We send the campaign and track open rates, click rates, and responses in real time. If something needs adjusting mid-campaign we handle it immediately.

05

You Get the Results Report

At the end of each cycle we send a full breakdown — how many contacts responded, how many booked, and what revenue was generated.

FAQ

Common Questions

What's the minimum number of contacts I need?

The minimum is 50 contacts to start with our Starter plan. The bigger your list the more potential bookings, but even 50 well-targeted contacts with the right offer can generate significant revenue.

What if my contact list is really old?

That's fine — most of our clients have lists that are 1–3 years old. We clean and segment the list first so we're only messaging people who are likely to respond. Older lists often surprise people with how many customers come back.

What counts as a "message"?

Each SMS or email sent to a contact counts as one message. So if you have 50 contacts and send them both an SMS and an email, that's 100 messages — which fits perfectly in the Starter plan.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients see responses within the first 24–48 hours of a campaign launching. Bookings typically start coming in within the first week. The first campaign usually pays for itself many times over.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely — you can upgrade at any time as your list grows or you want more campaigns per month. Many clients start on Starter, see results, and move to Growth or Pro within the first few months.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contracts. All plans are month-to-month and you can cancel anytime. We keep clients by getting results, not by locking them in.

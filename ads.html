FAQ

Common Questions

How much should I budget for ad spend?

We recommend a minimum of $500/month in ad spend to see consistent results. The more you spend the more leads you generate, but we'll help you find the right budget for your goals and market size.

Meta or Google — which is better for my business?

Both work well for different reasons. Google captures people actively searching for your services right now. Meta builds awareness and reaches customers before they need you. We'll recommend the right platform based on your business type and budget — or run both.

How long before I see leads coming in?

Most clients see their first leads within the first week of launch. Campaigns improve significantly over the first 30 days as the platforms optimize for your best audience.

Do I need a website for ads to work?

Not necessarily — we can run lead form ads on Meta that capture contact info directly without needing a website. Having one does improve results and builds trust, but it's not required to get started.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contracts. Month-to-month only. We recommend at least 60 days to see full results since the first month includes setup and learning phase, but you're never locked in.

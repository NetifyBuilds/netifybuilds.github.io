FAQ

Common Questions

Why is the bundle better than buying separately?

You save $397/month, but more importantly the two strategies work better together. Reactivation brings back customers who already trust you while ads bring in new ones — you're filling your calendar from both directions at the same time.

Do I need a customer list for the bundle?

Yes for the reactivation side — we need your existing customer database. If your list is small we can weight more effort toward the ads side while you build up more customer history.

How much ad spend budget do I need on top of the $997?

We recommend starting with at least $500/month in ad spend. This goes directly to Meta or Google — it's separate from our management fee and you control it completely.

Can I start with just one service and upgrade later?

Absolutely. Many clients start with reactivation only to prove results, then add ads management once they see the ROI. You can upgrade to the bundle at any time.

Is there a contract?

No contracts. Month-to-month only. We recommend sticking with it for at least 60 days to see the full compounding effect of both strategies running together, but you're never locked in.

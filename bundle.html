FAQ

Common Questions

Why is the bundle better than buying separately?

You save $99/month on every tier, but more importantly the two strategies work better together. Reactivation brings back contacts who already trust you while ads bring in new ones — you're filling your calendar from both directions at the same time.

What's the minimum number of contacts I need?

The minimum is 50 contacts to start with Bundle Starter. If your list is smaller we can weight more effort toward the ads side while you build up more contact history.

How much should I budget for ad spend on top of the bundle price?

We recommend a minimum of $500/month in ad spend. This goes directly to Meta or Google — it's separate from our management fee and you control it completely.

Can I start with one service and add the bundle later?

Absolutely. Many clients start with reactivation only to see results first, then upgrade to the bundle once they see the ROI. You can switch plans at any time.

Is there a contract?

No contracts. Month-to-month only. We recommend at least 60 days to see the full compounding effect of both services running together, but you're never locked in.
